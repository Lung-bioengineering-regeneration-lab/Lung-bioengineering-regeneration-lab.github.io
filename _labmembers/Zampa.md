I am Benedetta, and I recently completed my Master’s in Biomedical Engineering and Medical Physics at the Technical University of Munich, following my undergraduate studies at the University of Padua.

I am excited to join the Lung Bioengineering and Regeneration (LBR) group as an intern, where I am contributing to a capstone project centered on 3D bioprinting. This involves adapting a commercial 3D printer into a multi-extrusion bioprinter and optimizing bioinks to fabricate a three-layer model of the bronchus intermedius. Through this project, I contribute to the development of more representative airway models for studying lung biology and advancing translational applications.

I am particularly interested in the challenges of combining engineering design with biological constraints.
